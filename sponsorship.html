Sponsorship Opportunities

Become a Sponsor of Sanket'26

Sanket'26 offers an exceptional opportunity for organizations to showcase their commitment to technical education, innovation, and the advancement of electronics and communication engineering. As a sponsor, your organization will gain visibility among a diverse audience of students, faculty, and industry professionals from NIT Calicut and beyond.

With a strong focus on hands-on learning, emerging technologies, and collaborative knowledge sharing, Sanket serves as a vibrant platform to engage with future innovators and technical leaders shaping India's technology landscape.

Why Sponsor Sanket'26?

Brand Visibility

Gain prominent brand exposure through event materials, banners, social media, and direct interaction with students and faculty from premier institutions.

Talent Acquisition

Connect with talented engineering students and identify potential recruits for internships and full-time positions at your organization.

Industry Connect

Strengthen your connection with NIT Calicut's ECE department and contribute to the development of future technology leaders.

Sponsorship Benefits

What You Get

  • Logo placement on event website and promotional materials
  • Social media mentions and acknowledgment
  • Opportunity to set up recruitment/information stalls
  • Access to student database for recruitment purposes
  • Speaking opportunities during the event
  • Branding on event merchandise (for higher tiers)
  • Dedicated sessions or workshops (for Platinum sponsors)
  • Direct interaction with students and faculty

Benefits vary by sponsorship tier. Contact us for detailed sponsorship packages and customized opportunities.
