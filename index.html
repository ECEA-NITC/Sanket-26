A B O U T

Sanket'26
Annual Technical Symposium

Sanket'26 is the annual technical symposium organized by the Electronics and Communication Engineering Association (ECEA) at NIT Calicut. This flagship event brings together students, faculty, and industry professionals to celebrate innovation, creativity, and technical excellence in the field of electronics and communication engineering.

The symposium features a diverse range of activities including technical workshops, competitive events, guest lectures by industry experts, and interactive sessions. Sanket provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, learn about emerging technologies, and network with peers and professionals from the industry.

With its focus on hands-on learning and practical applications, Sanket'26 aims to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators, fostering a culture of technical excellence and collaborative growth within the ECE community at NIT Calicut.
A B O U T

ECEA, NIT Calicut

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Association (ECEA) is the official departmental association of the ECE Department at NIT Calicut. ECEA serves as a vibrant platform for students to engage in technical, cultural, and professional activities throughout the academic year.

ECEA organizes various events including technical workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, guest lectures by industry experts, and the annual technical symposium - Sanket. The association plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between academic learning and practical industry requirements, providing students with opportunities to enhance their technical skills and professional development.

Through its diverse initiatives and events, ECEA fosters a culture of innovation, collaboration, and excellence within the ECE community at NIT Calicut, preparing students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving field of electronics and communication engineering.

O U R  H O S T

NIT CALICUT

The National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) is an Institute of National Importance and one of India's leading institutions in engineering, technology, architecture, and management education. In the NIRF 2025 rankings, NIT Calicut was placed 21st in Engineering, reaffirming its stature among the top engineering institutions in the country.

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at NIT Calicut is one of the premier departments, known for its excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. The department offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, with state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities. Faculty members are actively involved in cutting-edge research in areas such as VLSI design, wireless communications, signal processing, embedded systems, and artificial intelligence.

The ECE department has a strong focus on industry collaboration and practical learning, regularly organizing workshops, seminars, and technical events through ECEA to ensure students are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern technology landscape.
